Chocolate Chip Cookies!

May 2018 (a looong time ago)

Bored in Uni Accomodation, my friends and I decided to bake a few hundred cookies. Stuck with far too many cookies, we then distributed them down Queen st to the people of Auckland Central. Recieveing smiles and hugs in return, there began the inspiration for the Lucky Dip Club.

The Plan

The ultimate goal is to have enough people supporting Lucky Dip Club to have a budget of $1000/month to do great things. In order to get there, I (Java) will front my own money, to create content that raises awareness about what we are doing to gather support from patreons, corprate sponsors and voluenteers.

We have some ideas of awesome things we could do, including...

  • Build community gardens
  • Distribute baking to the community
  • Provide a free concert on the beach
  • Provide large art installations by young artists
  • City-wide/NZ-wide Scavanger hunt!
  • Create tools for NZ digital content discovery
  • Facilitate markets for NZ creatives
  • Just give away a bunch of ice-cream in the park
  • Public classes for
    • Programming
